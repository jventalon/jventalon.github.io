About me

French Software Engineer, currently working on Java EE web applications at Capgemini in Clermont-Ferrand (France) since March 2017.

Interested in a wide range of new technologies, including Web and Mobile development and Virtual and Augmented Reality.

In 2017, I obtained a 'Diplôme d'ingénieur en Informatique', a 5-year degree equivalent to a Master of Engineering degree in Computer Science, from the University of Technology of Belfort-Montbéliard (France).

Now I work on web applications maintenance for Schneider-Electric in different business areas, such as quote configuration and after-sales service. My activities include customer needs analysis, functional and technical specifications writing, testing, as well as help to the offshore team members located in India.

I love to travel and discover new cultures. I think it is always very enriching to meet new people and work in multicultural and multidisciplinary teams. As part of my studies, I did four semesters abroad in different countries (United Kingdom, Peru, Canada and China), for studying and internships.

My native language is French and I can speak English and Spanish. I am also learning Chinese, German and Portuguese.
